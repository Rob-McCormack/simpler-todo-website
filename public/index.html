Why SimplerToDo

Everything you need.
Your data stays yours.

Five simple sections

Today, Tomorrow, Next 7 Days, Sometime, and Notes. Type 1.–5. to send tasks straight where they belong.

Open source & transparent

You can inspect the code, verify how data is stored, and fork it for your own workflow. Open source means no black box and no hidden lock-in.

Export in open formats

Export your tasks anytime to plain text, rich text, Markdown, or JSON. Keep a backup, move devices, or use your data in other tools whenever you want.

MUST DO + urgency markers

Keep one MUST DO task pinned at the top, and use ! or !! to mark urgency and keep important work visible.

Private by design

Your data lives in your browser on your device. No account required. No remote database required. No one else owns your task history.

Smart tags for people & places

Use @name for people, #home or #office for locations, and even ::global:: tags. Tap any tag to instantly filter your whole list.

Zero setup, instant start

Open the app and start typing. No signup required. Just you, your tasks, and a clean UI that stays out of the way.

Private by default

Your data is only on your device, and export is always available so you stay in control.

Keyboard‑first & blazing fast

Built for speed in the browser. Use Ctrl/⌘+K to open Quick Actions and keep your hands on the keyboard while you fly through your list.
