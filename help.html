Common questions

What is SimplerToDo?

SimplerToDo is a free, open-source to-do app with five sections (Today, Next, Waiting, Someday, Done), tags, reports, and strong keyboard support. Open the app at app.simplertodo.com.

Where is my data stored?

On your device only. There is no account and no cloud sync of your tasks through SimplerToDo. You can export your data anytime.

Do I need an account?

No. The app works without signing up.

How do I export my tasks?

Use the export options in the app to download or copy your tasks as plain text, rich text, Markdown, or JSON.

What are sections and tags?

Sections help you organize work across Today, Next, Waiting, Someday, and Done. Tags let you label and filter tasks across those sections.

Is SimplerToDo free?

Yes. The app is free and open source.

Is the source code available?

Yes — see the project links on the homepage for the repository and how to contribute.

How do I contact you?

Email simplertasks@gmail.com.

Ask a question

You can send a few questions per day (limit resets at midnight UTC). Questions are processed by Anthropic (Claude) to generate an answer. Do not paste secrets or private data.
