Why Simpler Tasks Lite

Everything you need.
Nothing to configure.

Five simple sections

Today, Tomorrow, Next 7 Days, Sometime, and Notes. Type 1.–5. to send tasks straight where they belong.

Open source & hackable

The core app is open source on GitHub. You can inspect the code, fork it, or build your own custom workflows on top of a single‑file HTML version.

Standalone desktop option

Prefer an app instead of a tab? Simpler Tasks Lite can be wrapped as a dedicated desktop app for macOS and Windows with a lightweight Electron shell.

Friendly chat support

Have a question or want help tuning your workflow? Reach out from the website and get friendly, human‑level chat support.

Sync across devices

Go Pro once and your tasks follow you between desktop, laptop, tablet, and phone — powered by Dexie Cloud for seamless, encrypted sync.

Smart tags for people & places

Use @name for people, #home or #office for locations, and even ::global:: tags. Tap any tag to instantly filter your whole list.

Zero setup, instant start

Open the app and start typing. No signup required for Lite. Just you, your tasks, and a clean UI that stays out of the way.

Private by default

Lite users never touch a server. Pro users get secure cloud sync. Your tasks stay yours.

Keyboard‑first & blazing fast

Built on React with Dexie. Use Ctrl/⌘+K to open Quick Actions and keep your hands on the keyboard while you fly through your list.
